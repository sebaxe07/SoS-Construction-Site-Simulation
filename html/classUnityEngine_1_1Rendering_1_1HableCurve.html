Initializes the curve.

Parameters
toeStrengthThe strength of the transition between the curve's toe and the curve's mid-section. A value of 0 results in no transition and a value of 1 results in a very hard transition.
toeLengthThe length of the curve's toe. Higher values result in longer toes and therefore contain more of the dynamic range.
shoulderStrengthThe strength of the transition between the curve's midsection and the curve's shoulder. A value of 0 results in no transition and a value of 1 results in a very hard transition.
shoulderLengthThe amount of f-stops to add to the dynamic range of the curve. This is how much of the highlights that the curve takes into account.
shoulderAngleHow much overshoot to add to the curve's shoulder.
gammaA gamma correction to the entire curve.
